# Dolphin Jump
Dolphin jump is a browser-based kids board game with a die. You roll a die to move along the board. The goal is to get to the finish first.

# Player tokens
* Each player has a dolphin token of different color.
* The colors are purple, pink, blue, and green.

# Starting screen
* The starting screen shows four dolphins of different colors, specified above.
* To the right of each dolphin, players can enter their age.
* If there is no age next to a dolphin, that dolphin is omitted from the game.

# Board design
* The board consists of 100 spots laid out sequentially, similar to Candy Land or Chutes & Ladders. 
* Start and finish spots have appropriate corresponding designs.
* Most spots are plain, however some are special and are described below.
* Special landing spots have extra decoration and indicate the special action via iconography and numbers


## Special landing spots
* Jump forward X spots - advance the dolphin forward the indicated number of spots.
* Jump back X spots - advance the dolphin backward the indicated number of spots.
* Switch spots - switch spots with the nearest dolphin.
* Roll again - roll the die again and advance the number of spots rolled.

# Rules

## Number of players
* 2-4 players

## Who goes first
* Youngest player goes first. Then players proceed in the order from youngest to oldest. Once all players go, the order restarts.
* Players enter their age on the starting screen when choosing their dolphin.

## Moving
* On player's turn, they roll the die by clicking on it. The die will generate a random roll (1-6).
* The player's dolphin advances the number rolled on the die.
* After advancing, if the dolphin lands on a special landing spot, the action indicated by the spot is performed.
* Then the turn ends and the next player goes.
* Two dolphins can be on the same spot.

## Winning
* The player whose dolphin gets to the finish spot first wins.
* When someone wins, the game shows the winning screen, which includes the dolphin which one with words "You Win!" above. There is a fire-work like effect with stars.

# Other requirements

## Rolling the die
* A standard 6-sided die, resulting in a random roll from 1 to 6.
* The die is shown somewhere on the screen. Rolling the die is done by clicking on the die. The animation shows the die rolling/spinning in place.
